The quick brown fox, named Foxy, jumps over the lazy dog! This dog, unlike others, didn't seem to mind. Why? Perhaps it's because the dog and the fox are friends; unusual friends, indeed.
In another story, a cat--whiskered and wise--watched from afar. The cat thought: "What an odd pair they make!" But, in the end, the cat decides to join them. And so, a new friendship began.
